# MILITARY INTELLIGENCE BIBLE: The Doctrine of Decisive Action
 
**Strategic Goal:** This volume distills the core methodologies from the U.S. Intelligence Community and Department of Defense textbooks. It mandates the use of **Joint Targeting**, **IPB**, and **Structured Analytics** to turn the **Omni** platform into a weaponized decision engine.
 
---
 
## 🏗️ 1. THE JOINT TARGETING CYCLE (JP 3-60)
*Focus: Selecting and prioritizing targets for decisive effect.*
 
### A. The 6-Phase Cycle
Every operation within **Omni** must follow these phases:
1. **End State & Commander's Objectives:** Define the "Why" (e.g., Neutralize rogue signal node).
2. **Target Development & Prioritization:** Identifying HVTs (High-Value Targets) and HPTs (High-Payoff Targets).
3. **Capabilities Analysis:** Selecting the right tool for the job (e.g., De-Auth vs. Jamming).
4. **Commander's Decision:** Final authorization for interdiction.
5. **Mission Planning & Execution:** The technical trigger (The System of Action).
6. **Assessment:** Post-action analysis to verify effect (The Assess phase).
 
### B. Dynamic Targeting (F2T2EA)
For time-sensitive targets, Omni utilizes the **Find, Fix, Track, Target, Engage, Assess** loop. This allows for the immediate interdiction of moving signals (e.g., a vehicle with a specific MAC address).

## 🏗️ 2. INTELLIGENCE PREPARATION OF THE BATTLEFIELD (IPB)
*Focus: Understanding the environment and the threat.*
 
### A. The 4-Step Process
1. **Define the Operational Environment (OE):** Mapping the 3D space, RF floor, and network topology.
2. **Describe Environmental Effects:** Analyzing how buildings (OAKOC) and infrastructure (ASCOPE) affect signals and movement.
3. **Evaluate the Threat:** Identifying threat signatures, doctrine, and hardware capabilities.
4. **Determine Threat COAs:** Predicting where the target will go next using neural "Pattern of Life" modeling.
 
### B. Tactical Decision Aids (Overlays)
Omni must support high-fidelity overlays:
- **MCOO:** Modified Combined Obstacle Overlay (Mapping blind spots and signal-dead zones).
- **Situation Template:** Visualizing predicted target locations over the next 5, 10, and 15 minutes.

## 🏗️ 3. STRUCTURED ANALYTIC TECHNIQUES (SATs)
*Focus: Mitigating cognitive bias and ensuring analytical integrity.*
 
### A. Analysis of Competing Hypotheses (ACH)
Agents must not settle on the first "Match." Omni must present the operator with multiple possibilities for a signal origin and rank them by consistency.
 
### B. Red Team Analysis
Before initiating a high-risk interdiction (Tier 3), Omni's `@tactical-logician` must perform a "Red Team" audit to predict potential adversary reactions and forensic signatures.
