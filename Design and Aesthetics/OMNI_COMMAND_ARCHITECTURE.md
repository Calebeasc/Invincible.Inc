### 1. The "Titanium" Visual Language
Omni utilizes the **Blueprint 6** framework principles, prioritizing perceptual uniformity via the **OKLCH color space**.
- **Core Background:** Deep, desaturated charcoal: `oklch(20% 0.02 260)`.
- **Semantic Intent Colors:** 
    - **Blue:** Information/Selection (Lattice Handshake).
    - **Neon Green:** Success/Friendly units.
    - **Bright Amber:** Warnings/Anomalies.
    - **Stark Red:** Danger/Hostile threats.
- **Typography:** High-density sans-serif (Inter/Roboto Mono). Text is small (11px-13px) but high-contrast to maximize "Pixels of Insight."
 
### 2. The Gaia 3D Map & Video Draping
The centerpiece is a live, high-definition 3D Map (Gaia) where real-time drone feeds are "projected" or "draped" directly onto the 3D terrain.
- **Contextual Alignment:** Video feeds are stretched and aligned over virtual buildings, showing exactly what the sensor sees in the context of the street layout.
- **Temporal Tracks:** Every signal has a "Tail" (History), showing a faint line of its path over the last 10 minutes.
- **Custom Shaders:** Apply "Wireframe" or "Thermal" shaders to building geometry for a tactical drone-feed aesthetic.
 
### 3. Interaction: The Logic of Action
- **Object Drawers:** Side panels that provide deep dives into the Ontology. No pop-ups allowed.
- **Dependency Graphs:** Visualizing the logic lines between hardware nodes and AI agents.
- **The Joint Targeting Cycle (F2T2EA):** All target interactions follow the **Find, Fix, Track, Target, Engage, Assess** loop. This ensures that every interdiction is deliberate and assessed for effect.
- **IPB Overlays:** Support for specialized tactical overlays:
    - **MCOO:** Mapping physical/electromagnetic obstacles and blind spots.
    - **ASCOPE:** Providing human-centric context (People, Organizations, Events) to target signatures.
- **Heartbeat Indicators:** Every node in the lattice has a tiny pulsing green dot indicating active data transmission.
